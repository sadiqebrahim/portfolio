Sadiq Siraj Ebrahim

I am currently working as a Scientist at the Centre for Development of Telematics (C-DOT), India. Prior to this, my research focused on computer vision and its applications in face recognition, age transformation, explainable AI, and identity-preserving generation. I have also developed several deep learning pipelines for tasks such as deforestation detection from satellite imagery, event detection, and neural network implementation on FPGA hardware.
